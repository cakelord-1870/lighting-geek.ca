Laser Lights

Laser is useful in entertainment because the coherent nature of laser lights allows a narrow beam to be produced, which allows the use of optical scanning to draw patterns or images on walls, ceilings or other surfaces including theatrical smoke and fog without refocusing for the differences in distance, as is common with video projection. LightingGeek carries the most popular lines of Laser Stage Lights with laser power ranging from 50mW to 9W. We cover from entry-level laser light for home parties to professional level lasers for big concerts, nightclubs and laser stage lighting applications.

DJ laser lights is generally used by mobile disco DJs and in most modern nightclubs and many late-night bars. Integrated with computer chip, modern laser disco lights can have various laser lighting effects.
